Everything You Should Know About Maternity Pay

Preparing for maternity leave goes beyond choosing baby names and nursery colors. Financial security during your time away from work is essential. That’s where a maternity pay calculator becomes a powerful resource.

Not all maternity leave is paid the same. Depending on your job, country, and benefits, your income may drop significantly. By using a calculator, you avoid guesswork and can build a smarter financial plan tailored to your personal situation.
